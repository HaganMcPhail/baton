Some words about us

Our desire is to engage women cross-generationally in their faith through missional living. Baton’s mission is to connect, equip, and engage women in intentional mentorship.

  • Connect - We use relevant issues to connect women together.
  • Equip - We equip them with various resources to get the job done.
  • Engage - Participants engage together and build meaningful relationships.


Mentors

Are you are contemplating whether you could be a mentor or not? Do you know Jesus? Have you walked with Him long enough to hear and practice the truth of His word? Baton is for you.

Mentees
