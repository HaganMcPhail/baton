about us

Our desire is to engage women cross-generationally in their faith through missional living. Baton’s mission is to connect, equip, and engage women in intentional mentorship.

Connect - Baton will provide local and relevant women to connect with based on what you are looking for. Get connected online but meet in real life!

About BATON

Some of us walk down foggy roads, looking for clarity and direction. What if we could walk with someone who can shed light and experience on the journey?

Baton serves two kinds of people: the person who is looking for direction or encouragement -and- the person who is looking for an avenue to share the life lessons they’ve learned.

While there are many books and resources to equip us in the Christian life, many people lack the one-on-one intentional mentorship that spurs real growth. Books can’t replace real, raw conversation. Superficial relationships can’t speak into the core of who you are. Baton is giving you permission to be real. What are your struggles, your passions, and the tensions in your life?

We will match you with women who have walked in similar roles, gone through similar experiences or pains, and are passionate about helping you navigate through this world as the woman God has created and called you to be.


Is Baton for You?

Maybe you need immediate, Godly advice. Maybe you want mentorship for a short season. Or maybe you are looking to be fully known and held accountable for the long run. Whether it’s seeking someone who’s been through a similar struggle, learning from life experience in roles such as a being wife or mom, or finding someone to fuel the passion in your heart in areas of business or creativity, God has equipped the body of Christ to speak truth and life into all of these situations. He has given us his Word, Holy Spirit, and each other--what a precious gift!

Do you know Jesus? Have you walked with Him long enough to hear and practice the truth of His word? Your experience as: a believer, a wife, a mom, a single woman, a conqueror of obstacles, a learner from past mistakes, a seeker of God’s will...those are the threads God has weaved in you. Those experiences are not just for yourself--they are to encourage and guide others as we pursue His holiness and our sanctification.

Answer the Call.
Pass it on.


Our Name - BATON

We all have a race to run. It might be your role as a mom, your journey through a struggle, or a passion to use your story to inspire others. But we aren’t meant to run alone—we are called to run as a team. It is a world of a difference to have someone who can point out the potholes that they tripped on or encourage you up a difficult hill.

God calls us to step out of our comfort zone and step into areas that require much of us. He has not only equipped us with his Word and Holy Spirit, but with His people. We can look to and learn from others who have run a similar race.

In any relay, the success doesn’t just depend on each individual’s ability to run well. It also hinges on the passing of the baton. The passer must be intentional to position the baton in a way that sets up the receiver for success. It can’t be thrown carelessly and then expected to be caught. There is coaching, practice, and training involved. The next runner must also be ready and prepared. Once it’s in their hand, they can’t just stand there. They need to run.

Let’s run as a team. Let’s equip the next generation by making sure we are intentional in the pass off. If you are ‘waiting' for your turn to run, know that you must shift in how you understand your position. You are training, building strength, and preparing. How do you prepare for a race? By running. Live in anticipation and expectancy-not in a stand still. It’s not a matter of if you will be passed the baton, but when.

What will you do with it?
